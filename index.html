About Me

Hi, I'm Armen. I'm a student at Northeastern University in San Jose, where I'm currently working toward earning a Master's degree in Computer Science. Before I entered this program, I was a classically-trained pianist living and freelancing in New York City! I've found there are actually many similarities between music and computer science. Both require using intuition and logic to solve problems, and also offer the opportunity to create new and exciting things. While it still brings me great joy to play the piano and compose music, I'm thrilled to be applying my creativity in new ways while learning lots of exciting skills along the way! To learn more about my story as a classical pianist, please visit my music page. You can follow my progress in my computer science journey at my tech page.
