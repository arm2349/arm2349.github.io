My Musical Background

Born into a musical family, I was exposed to classical music from a young age. I first started playing the piano at 4 years old, and it's been a major part of my life ever since. Throughout my musical career, I've performed in numerous concert venues around the world, including Carnegie Hall. I've also competed in piano competitions at the state, national, and international levels, earning top prizes several times. There are three degrees I earned in my musical education:

  • Indiana University, Bachelor of Science in Music and an Outside Field (BSOF): Piano Performance, Sociology
  • Indiana University, Performer Diploma (PD): Piano Performance
  • The Juilliard School, Master of Music (MM): Piano Performance

While I still perform occasionally, in more recent years I've spent more time teaching, composing, and accompanying other musicians. Though most of my time is now committed to my computer science education, I've been able to maintain a light schedule of flexible musical work in the meantime as I transition toward my new career. If you're interested in taking piano lessons, please feel free to contact me.
